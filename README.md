# Ecommerce Colorful Store Project Structure

## Project Sections

### Home
- Overview of the store and featured products.

### Shop
- Displays various products for sale.
- Filters for categories and price ranges.

### About Us
- Information about the company and its mission.

### Resources
- Links to articles, guides, and support.

### Testimonials
- Customer reviews and feedback.

### Blog
- Articles related to shopping tips, product features, and news.

### Contact
- Contact form and company contact details.

### Footer
- Quick links, social media icons, and copyright information.
